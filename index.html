Privacy Policy
Last updated: April 2025 · GridCoffer

1. Who We Are

GridCoffer converts PDF files to Excel spreadsheets. This policy covers personal information handling under Canada's PIPEDA.

2. What We Collect

PDF content: Never collected, stored, or transmitted. All processing occurs locally in your browser using PDF.js and SheetJS. Your document never leaves your device.

Account & billing (Pro): Name, email, and payment data are handled by Stripe. GridCoffer receives only a subscription status confirmation — never raw payment credentials.

Usage analytics: None at this time.

3. PIPEDA Compliance

  • Accountability: GridCoffer is responsible for all personal information under its control.
  • Purpose: Files are processed solely to perform conversion. No secondary use.
  • Consent: By using GridCoffer you consent to transient local processing of your document.
  • Limiting collection: No document content is collected.
  • Safeguards: Processing in-browser means no server-side exposure.
  • Openness: This policy is publicly accessible at all times.
  • Individual access: Contact us to access or correct your account data.

4. Transient Processing Note

Even transient in-memory handling of documents containing personal information (names, financial data, health records) is covered by PIPEDA. GridCoffer's local-only model means this data is never accessible to us.

5. Third Parties

  • Stripe: Payment processing. stripe.com/privacy
  • PDF.js (Mozilla): Runs locally. No data sent to Mozilla.
  • SheetJS: Runs locally. No data sent externally.
  • Tesseract.js: OCR for Pro. Runs locally. No data sent externally.

6. Contact

For privacy concerns or data access requests, please reach out through the GridCoffer website contact page.
Office of the Privacy Commissioner of Canada: priv.gc.ca
